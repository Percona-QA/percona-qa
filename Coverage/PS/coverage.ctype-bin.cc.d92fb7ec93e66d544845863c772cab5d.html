31 @file ctype-bin.cc
32 The “binary” pseudo-charset. binary is special in that it's not really
33 a character set; conversions from another charset _to_ binary means
34 “erase the charset information” and conversions _from_ binary to another
35 charset means “interpret these bytes as the destination charset”.
36 In other words, in no event are the bytes changed; you can think of
37 binary as the charset equivalent of void * that you can cast through.
38 Needless to say, this also means that using it can be rather dangerous.
39 */
